About Me

Welcome to my personal website!

I am an energy system modeler and a Ph.D. candidate in Copernicus Institute of Sustainable Development at Utrecht University, supervised by Dr. Jing Hu, Prof. Detlef van Vuuren and Dr. Vinzenz Koning. More information about my profile at Utrecht University can be found here.

I am also collaborating with Dr. Gang He at the Deep Policy Lab (City University of New York). Previously, I worked with Dr. Wei He at the STAR Lab (King’s College London).

I hold an M.Sc. in Electronic Information and a B.Eng. in Electrical Engineering & Automation, both from Xi’an Jiaotong University (西安交通大学). I was also a visiting student at King’s College London during 2024 summer.

Research

My research focuses on energy system modeling, climate policy, and emerging low-carbon technologies such as hydrogen, fusion, and space-based solar power etc. I leverage energy system modeling to develop actionable strategies that addressing the complexities of climate change and decarbonization. I am also interested in electricity markets, resilience and climate risk, and integrating machine learning into energy system analysis.

I have previously worked on power system analysis and linear optical simulations. (Yes, two fields that rarely meet at conferences.)
